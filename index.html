About Our Team

Team 10477 Rapid Robots is a passionate group of 10 middle school students from grades 7–8, united by curiosity and creativity in STEM. For over 10 years, we have embraced challenges, engineered advanced robots, and grown as leaders, problem-solvers, and innovators.

Our journey is not just about building robots; it’s about building character. Each season, we push our limits, learn from failures, celebrate victories, and share knowledge with our community. Rapid Robots inspires younger students, promotes teamwork, and fosters a lifelong love of science, technology, engineering, and math. Join us as we continue to innovate, compete, and make a difference!
